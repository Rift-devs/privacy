Privacy Policy

How Rift collects, stores, and protects your information.

Support ServerTerms of Service

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Rift values your privacy. We only collect and process information necessary to provide, maintain, and improve the bot's functionality.

2. Data We Collect

  • Discord User IDs
  • Usernames
  • Display names
  • Avatar URLs
  • Guild IDs
  • Channel IDs
  • Role IDs
  • Command usage data
  • Server configuration settings

3. How We Use Data

Data is used for bot configuration, moderation systems, permission management, command processing, security, diagnostics, and service improvements.

4. Data Storage & Retention

Data is stored only as long as required for Rift to function. Server configuration data may remain while Rift is installed and may be removed when no longer needed.

5. Data Sharing

Rift does not sell, rent, or trade user data. Information may only be disclosed when required by law or to maintain service security and integrity.

6. Third-Party Services

Rift operates through Discord and may use hosting, database, or cloud infrastructure providers to deliver its services.

7. Data Security

Reasonable safeguards are implemented to protect stored data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

8. User Rights

Users may request information about stored data, request corrections, or request deletion of applicable data where permitted.

9. Children's Privacy

Rift is not intended for individuals below Discord's minimum required age.

10. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of Rift after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact the Rift Development Team through the official support server.
