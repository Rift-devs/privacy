Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Rift values your privacy. We only collect and process information necessary to provide, maintain, and improve the bot's functionality.

2. Data We Collect

Rift may collect and process the following information:

  • Discord User IDs
  • Usernames
  • Display Names
  • Avatar URLs
  • Guild (Server) IDs
  • Channel IDs
  • Role IDs
  • Command usage data
  • Server configuration settings

3. How We Use Data

Collected information is used solely for operating Rift, including:

  • Bot configuration
  • Moderation systems
  • Permission management
  • Command processing
  • Security and abuse prevention
  • Diagnostics and troubleshooting
  • Service improvements

4. Data Storage & Retention

Data is stored only as long as necessary for Rift to function. Server configuration data may remain while Rift is installed and may be removed when no longer required.

5. Data Sharing

Rift does not sell, rent, or trade user data. Information may only be disclosed when required by law or when necessary to maintain the security and integrity of the service.

6. Third-Party Services

Rift operates through Discord and may rely on third-party hosting, database, cloud infrastructure, analytics, or security providers to deliver its services.

7. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational safeguards are implemented to protect stored information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

8. User Rights

Users may request information about stored data, request corrections, or request deletion of applicable data where permitted by law and technically feasible.

9. Children's Privacy

Rift is not intended for individuals below Discord's minimum required age to use the Discord platform.

10. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of Rift after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, please contact the Rift Development Team through the official support server.
